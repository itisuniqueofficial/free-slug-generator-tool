Frequently Asked Questions

What is a slug?

A slug is the part of a URL that identifies a particular page on a website in a readable form. It usually appears after the domain name and helps improve SEO.

Why is it important to optimize a slug?

Optimizing slugs improves the readability and relevance of a URL, which helps search engines rank your page higher in search results.

Can I use special characters in my slug?

No, this tool automatically removes special characters and replaces spaces with hyphens to ensure your slug is URL-friendly.
